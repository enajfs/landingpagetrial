Common questions

You have questions. We have honest answers.

Will HeadStart read all my email? +
Yes — to do its job, HeadStart connects via OAuth and processes incoming messages. We never store raw email content longer than necessary, and you can revoke access instantly.
Is my data secure? +
End‑to‑end encryption in transit, no data selling. The architecture supports on‑prem deployment when you’re ready. Your mailbox facts stay yours.
What if I miss the 06:55 notification? +
No problem — yesterday’s pending items appear in tomorrow’s briefing automatically. You can also open the briefing link any time for an on‑demand delta view.
Does HeadStart modify my inbox or send emails? +
Never. HeadStart only reads your mailbox. No auto‑archiving, labeling, or sending on your behalf. Your inbox stays exactly as you left it.
Which email platforms are supported? +
Microsoft 365 (Outlook) and Google Workspace (Gmail). Both integrated via secure APIs — SMB owners on either platform are covered.
How does follow‑through tracking actually work? +
Deterministic check: for each action‑required email, HeadStart looks into Sent and Drafts for a reply. “Draft started” / “responded” are facts, not probabilistic. No AI guesswork.
